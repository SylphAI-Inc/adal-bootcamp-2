Our Story

Sound you can feel.

Lumen began in a garage in Oakland with a stubborn belief: that great sound shouldn't require a compromise between science and soul.

How we started

Two engineers, one reference curve, zero shortcuts

In 2019, acoustic engineers Maya Ruiz and Devan Kapoor were frustrated that the headphones they could afford never matched what they heard in the mastering studio. So they built their own — measuring, listening, and re-measuring until the gear disappeared and only the music remained.

Seven years later, Lumen is still small, still obsessive, and still tuned by the same two ears that started it all — now backed by a team who share the obsession.
