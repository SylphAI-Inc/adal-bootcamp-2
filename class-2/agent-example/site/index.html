Every order ships free and includes a 30-day, no-questions-asked return window. If your Lumens aren't perfect for you, send them back for a full refund — we even cover return shipping.

How long is the warranty?+

Every pair comes with a two-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. Ear cushions and cables are replaceable, and we offer free lifetime acoustic tune-ups at any Lumen service center.

How does Adaptive ANC actually work?+

A six-microphone array samples your environment 50,000 times a second and our chip generates a precise inverse waveform to cancel it — up to -42dB. Flip to Transparency mode and the room comes right back in.
